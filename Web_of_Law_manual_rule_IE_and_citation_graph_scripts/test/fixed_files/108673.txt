    In the Matter of the Disbarment of Seymour R. THALER. No. D-9.
    Supreme Court of the United States
    February 20, 1973
    
      11
      It is ordered that Seymour R. Thaler of New York, New York, be suspended from the practice of law in this Court and that a rule issue returnable within forty days requiring him to show cause why he should not be disbarred from the practice of law in this Court.
